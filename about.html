Our Story

Serving Customers Since 2014

Since 2014, YUS Water Solutions LLP has focused on one problem: helping people manage hard water and access better everyday drinking water, without relying on salt, chemicals or high-maintenance filtration systems.

What began as a focused effort in electromagnetic water treatment has grown into a company trusted by homeowners, hoteliers, hospitals, farmers and industrial buyers who need dependable water technology that simply works in the background.
