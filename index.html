Water, Tuned to Your Needs

The YUS Alkaline Water Machine uses electrolysis to separate purified water into two streams — an alkaline stream at your selected pH, and an acidic stream suited to different, non-drinking applications.

Alkaline Output — Drinking

pH 8 – 8.5 — Everyday Drinking Water

A gentle starting point, close to lightly alkaline mineral water — suited to everyday hydration for the whole family.

Acidic Output — Non-Drinking

pH 6 – 6.5 — Plants & Household Rinsing

Mildly acidic — a gentle choice for watering plants or general household rinsing.
