The Technology

How Electromagnetic Softening Works

Instead of removing minerals with salt or chemicals, the YUS Water Softener applies an electromagnetic field around your existing pipework. This influences scale-forming particles so they're less likely to bond to pipe walls and appliance surfaces as water flows through your system.

  • No chemicals
  • No salt
  • No filters to replace
  • One-time installation
