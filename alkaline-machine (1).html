YUS Alkaline Water Machine — Water, Tuned to Your Needs

An electrolysis-based water ionizer that produces water across four selectable alkaline ranges (8–10.5), each simultaneously paired with a corresponding acidic range (4.5–6.5), giving you full control over your everyday water.

How It Works

Electrolysis, Explained Simply

The machine uses electrolysis to separate purified water into two streams — an alkaline stream at your selected pH, and an acidic stream suited to different, non-drinking applications.

Alkaline Output — Drinking

pH 8 – 8.5 — Everyday Drinking Water

A gentle starting point, close to lightly alkaline mineral water — suited to everyday hydration for the whole family.

Acidic Output — Non-Drinking

pH 6 – 6.5 — Plants & Household Rinsing

Mildly acidic — a gentle choice for watering plants or general household rinsing.

Water suitability depends on source water quality. Please follow manufacturer guidance for your household's needs.
